Codespace is currently in **private beta**. You need an invite code to create an account.

## Sign up

1. Go to [codespace.sh/signup](https://codespace.sh/signup)
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Invite code** — provided by your team or Codespace
   - **Name** — your display name
   - **Email** — used for login and notifications
   - **Password** — minimum 12 characters

### Password requirements

Your password must be at least 12 characters. A strength indicator shows whether you've included:

- Uppercase letter
- Lowercase letter
- Number
- Special character

## Verify your email

After signing up, you'll receive a verification email. Click the link to activate your account.

<Warning>
  You must verify your email before you can sign in. If you try to log in before verifying, you'll see a prompt to check your inbox.
</Warning>

### Didn't receive the email?

- Check your spam or junk folder
- Use the **Resend verification email** option on the login page
- Make sure you entered the correct email address during signup

## What's next?

Once your email is verified, [sign in](/getting-started/sign-in) and [create your first instance](/getting-started/create-your-first-instance).
