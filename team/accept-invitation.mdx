---
title: Accept an invitation
description: Join a team instance via invitation link
---

When someone invites you to their Codespace instance, you'll receive an email with a link to accept.

## Accepting an invitation

1. Click the invitation link in your email
2. The acceptance page shows:
   - The instance name
   - Your assigned role
   - Who invited you
3. Click **Accept Invitation**
4. You're redirected to the instance dashboard

## Account requirements

### Already have a Codespace account

If you're signed in with the matching email, you can accept immediately.

If you're signed in with a **different email**, you'll see a mismatch warning. Sign out and sign back in with the correct email to accept.

### New to Codespace

If you don't have an account yet:

1. Click the link to **Sign up**
2. Create your account using the same email the invitation was sent to
3. Verify your email
4. Return to the invitation link and accept

<Warning>
  You must use the same email address the invitation was sent to. Accepting with a different email is not supported.
</Warning>

## After accepting

Once you accept, you'll appear as a member in the instance's team list. You can:

- View the instance dashboard
- Create and manage your own workspaces
- Access the Coder dashboard at the instance domain
