This guide walks you through the complete flow from sign-up to a running workspace.

## 1. Create your account

Codespace is in private beta. You need an **invite code** to sign up.

1. Go to [codespace.sh/signup](https://codespace.sh/signup)
2. Enter your invite code, name, email, and a password (minimum 12 characters)
3. Check your email for a verification link and click it

<Note>
  If you don't receive the verification email, check your spam folder. You can also resend it from the login page.
</Note>

## 2. Create your first instance

1. From the [dashboard](https://codespace.sh/dashboard), click **New Instance**
2. Enter a **name** for your instance (e.g., "My Team")
3. Choose a **subdomain** — this becomes your instance URL: `your-subdomain.codespace.sh`
4. Click **Create Instance**

## 3. Wait for provisioning

Codespace provisions a dedicated Coder deployment for you. This typically takes **2–5 minutes**.

You can watch the progress on the instance detail page — the **Events** tab shows each provisioning step in real time.

<Info>
  Your instance transitions through these states: **Pending** → **Provisioning** → **Running**. The dashboard auto-refreshes every few seconds.
</Info>

## 4. Open your Coder dashboard

Once your instance shows **Running**:

1. Click **Open Coder** to go to your Coder dashboard at `your-subdomain.codespace.sh`
2. Use the **admin credentials** shown on the instance detail page to sign in
3. You're now in Coder — create your first workspace from the available templates

## 5. Invite your team (optional)

1. Go to your instance's **Settings** page
2. Under **Team Members**, enter an email and click **Invite**
3. Your teammate receives an email with a link to join
