Codespace offers three subscription tiers to match your team's needs.

## Plan comparison

| Feature | Solo | Team | Custom |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| **Workspaces per user** | 5 | 20 | Unlimited |
| **Total workspaces** | 10 | 100 | Custom |
| **CPU hours/month** | 720 | 2,880 | Custom |
| **Memory GB-hours/month** | Included | Included | Custom |
| **Storage** | Included | Included | Custom |
| **Team members** | 1 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Custom domains** | No | Yes | Yes |
| **Dedicated support** | No | No | Yes |

## Solo plan

Best for individual developers:

- 5 workspaces per user, 10 total
- 720 CPU hours per month (~1 workspace running 24/7)
- Ideal for personal projects and experimentation

## Team plan

Best for teams and organizations:

- 20 workspaces per user, 100 total
- 2,880 CPU hours per month (~4 workspaces running 24/7)
- Unlimited team members
- Custom domain support

## Custom plan

For organizations with specific requirements:

- Unlimited workspaces
- Custom resource limits
- Dedicated support
- SLA guarantees
- Contact sales for pricing

## Resource overages

If you exceed your plan's included resources:

- An **Overage** badge appears on the usage meters in your instance settings
- Overage usage is billed at the end of the billing period
- Usage is tracked per billing cycle — it resets when a new period begins

<Tip>
  Monitor your usage from the instance Settings page. If you're consistently hitting overages, upgrading to the next tier is usually more cost-effective.
</Tip>

## Changing plans

To upgrade or change your plan, visit the instance Settings page. For downgrades or custom arrangements, contact support.
