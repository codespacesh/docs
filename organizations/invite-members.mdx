title: Invite members
description: Add members to your organization
---

Any organization member can invite others to join their organization via email.

## Send an invitation

1. Go to your organization detail page
2. Click **Settings**
3. Under **Members**, enter the person's email address
4. Click **Invite**

The invitee receives an email with a link to join your organization.

## Pending invitations

After sending an invitation, it appears in the **Pending Invitations** list with:

- The email address
- When the invitation expires

Invitations expire after a set period. If an invitation expires, you can send a new one.

## Revoke an invitation

If you need to cancel a pending invitation before it's accepted:

1. Go to organization **Settings** → **Members**
2. Find the invitation in the **Pending Invitations** list
3. Click **Revoke**

The invitation link becomes invalid immediately.

## What the invitee sees

When someone clicks the invitation link:

- If they already have a CodeWire account, they can accept directly
- If they don't have an account, they'll be prompted to sign up first
- The invitation page shows your organization name, the assigned role, and who sent the invite

See [Accept invitation](/organizations/accept-invitation) for the full invitee experience.

<Note>
  The invitee must sign in with the same email address the invitation was sent to. Signing in with a different email will show a mismatch warning.
</Note>
